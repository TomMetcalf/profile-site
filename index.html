About Tom Seager

Tom Seager is an artist and animator based in Braunton, Devon, UK, who creates loop based surf animations using a variety of techniques, inclucing stop-motion to rotoscoping.

Tom Seager Art has created animations of the following surfers

  • Tia Blanco
  • Kelly Slater
  • Jordy Smith
  • Maud Le Car

Bringing Art to Life

Using AR technology (Augmented Reality) a still image can be magically brought to life.

Animations are created using the following tools

  1. Adobe Photoshop
  2. Adobe After Effects
  3. Procreate
  4. Rough Animator
  5. Stop Motion Studio
